About

Computer Department




The computer department of universal college of engineering focuses on making our students keen about the core subjects of the branch. The branch encourages our students to be tech savvy and aware of this very dynamic field. The conduct of department is designed to motivate students interest into various aspects of the field and gives platform to stage their individuality and creativity in the form of projects.

In terms of learning aspects, various workshops are held in multiple frequencies throughout the year under the banner of CSI and CESA(and much more) which encourages students and motivates them to push their boundaries. There are multiple competitions held every year in college organised tech fests like VYRO and TANTROTSAV which helps students measure their knowledge and upgrade their practical skills. The department also encourages students to compete on a national level under the guidance of our well experienced faculties.
