@Vyro:-

Vyro is the official techfest of Universal College of Engineering, Vasai. Held every even sem it is the biggest festival of the college with numerous technical as well as non technical events which gives insight to the students of their technical skills and help them prepare batter for the national level competitions. Vyro also conducts non technical events so that students can relax from their routine and experience something different.

❮ ❯




@CSI:-

CSI stands for computer society of India. Its a national level body present in many engineering colleges of the nation with main focus and emphasis on giving students out of the syllabus but crucial knowledge. It organises many workshops as well as tech talks through out the whole year and encourages students to know more about the different fields branching out of their core branch. The topics popularly covered by the body is cyber security, AI, Data Science, Robotics, etc.

❮ ❯




@Tantrotsav:-

Tantrotsav is the technical event held every odd sem in Universal college of engineering, Vasai. Main aim of this event is to prepare the students of UCoE for the IIT held competitions so that they can have winning chance when they participate and fill well prepared. This event same as Vyro, has both technical/non technical events and helps students evaluate and improve their tech skills. Comps events include coding competitons such as code red, Web mania and AI hack.

❮ ❯




@CESA:-

CESA stands for COMPUTER ENGINEERING STUDENTS ASSOCIATION and it is a student body developed by the comps faculty of universal college of engineering to encourage students to share their technical knowledge with their peers. The body stands to motivate students to go beyond their curriculum and learn something different and share their knowledge with their friends and colleagues. The organization stands to make students future leaders.

❮ ❯




@National Events:-

The students of Universal college of engineering participate in various national events throughout the whole year. The students of UCoE have even won competitions in prestigious IIT's. For students of computer department there are various national level events happening in every parts of India. From hackathons to web development to real life application projects the competitions never stop. Many students participate in IOT and Robotics events too. The faculty of computer department is always supportive of our students competing on a national level.
